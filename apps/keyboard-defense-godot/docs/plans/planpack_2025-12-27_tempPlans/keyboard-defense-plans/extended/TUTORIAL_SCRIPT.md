# Tutorial Script (Typing-First)

## LANDMARK: Tutorial goals
- Teach controls without requiring mouse.
- Introduce typing prompts gradually.
- Avoid punishing the player during tutorial battles.

## Structure
- Segment 1: Warmup typing and basic HUD
- Segment 2: Targets and streak buffs
- Segment 3: Pause, resume, and retreat
- Segment 4: Rewards and upgrades
- Segment 5: First exploration event

## Segment 1: Warmup typing and basic HUD
Player sees a prompt:
- "Type: as"
Then:
- "Type: sad"
- "Type: add"

Acceptance:
- The HUD shows target and typed text.
- The player can clear the warmup without time pressure.

## Segment 2: Targets and streak buffs
Prompts introduce a short streak:
- "Type: ask"
- "Type: sash"

Acceptance:
- A streak indicator appears.
- Buff tooltip appears (typing power or slow threat).

## Segment 3: Pause, resume, and retreat
Prompts:
- "Press Esc to pause"
- "Press Resume to continue"
- "Press Retreat to exit the battle"

Acceptance:
- Pause menu is keyboard navigable.
- Retreat cleanly exits to results or map.

## Segment 4: Rewards and upgrades
Prompts:
- "Review your accuracy and WPM"
- "Spend gold on an upgrade"

Acceptance:
- Rewards screen shows accuracy and WPM.
- Upgrade selection works with keyboard only.

## Segment 5: First exploration event
- Spawn a POI near the first map node.
- Prompt: "Explore the marker"
- Present a short event with two choices (code and phrase modes available).

Completion rewards
- Unlock Practice Yard mode (free typing drills).
- Unlock a cosmetic banner (visual only).
