# Research summary: Super Fantasy Kingdom - gameplay pillars to emulate (not copy)

Purpose: capture high-level loop patterns to inform Keyboard Defense without
reusing any proprietary content or layouts.

## Observed core loop (high level)
- Roguelite runs with persistent unlocks.
- Planning and upgrades between defense phases.
- Exploration expands available options.
- Defense phase rewards preparation and resource management.

## Design takeaways (portable patterns)
1. Tight planning vs defense pacing.
2. Economy, upgrades, and combat interdependence.
3. Persistent progression keeps failure motivating.
4. Simple inputs, deeper strategy.

## Guardrails
- Do not copy UI layouts, text, art, or naming.
- Use original content and typing-first interaction.
