# Trailer Beat Sheet (60-75 seconds)

## 0-5s: Hook
- Fast cuts: typing prompt -> building appears -> night wave hits

## 5-20s: Premise
- "Command your kingdom by typing."
- Show day planning: gather/build/upgrade.

## 20-40s: The Loop
- Explore to find POIs and resources.
- Show threat cards modifying the night.
- Emphasize that typing affects speed/efficiency.

## 40-55s: Progression
- Show unlocks/perks and adaptive difficulty.

## 55-75s: CTA
- Wishlist / play demo / join community
- End on a dramatic night defense moment + title card

## Audio Notes
- Keep prompt sounds subtle (avoid fatigue).
- Use rising tension into night defense.
