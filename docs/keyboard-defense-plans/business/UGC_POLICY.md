# User-Generated Content (UGC) Policy (Optional)

Only apply this if you support:
- custom wordpacks
- custom events/POIs
- mod packs

## Allowed
- Original wordpacks focused on typing skills.
- Non-offensive content suitable for general audiences.

## Not Allowed
- Hate speech, harassment, slurs.
- Sexual content involving minors (strictly prohibited).
- Instructions for wrongdoing.
- Personal data (names, addresses, phone numbers).
- Malware or obfuscated scripts.

## Moderation
- Provide a report mechanism.
- Remove violating content promptly.
- Maintain a transparent enforcement log (aggregate).

## Safety Defaults
- UGC disabled by default for public builds, or sandboxed.
- Content validation and strict schema enforcement.
- Optional "family-friendly" filter.
