# Sprint Planning Guide (Lightweight)

## Inputs
- Milestone acceptance criteria
- Risk register (highest risk first)
- Playtest findings

## Planning Steps (30-60 minutes)
1. Pick 3-6 stories that move the milestone forward.
2. Confirm each story meets Definition of Ready.
3. Decompose into tasks only as needed.
4. Identify test plan and harness updates.

## Execution Rules
- Do not start more than 2 stories simultaneously.
- Always leave time for stabilization and playtest fixes.
- Cut scope, not quality gates.

## End-of-Sprint Checklist
- Internal playtest build cut
- Changelog updated
- Top issues triaged
