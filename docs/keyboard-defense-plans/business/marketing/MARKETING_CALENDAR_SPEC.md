# Marketing Calendar Spec (Keyboard Defense)

This document replaces the marketing calendar spreadsheet with a lightweight
text-first plan.

## Suggested timeline

### Pre-announcement
- 1 short teaser (concept art or UI mock)
- 1 devlog describing the typing-first vision

### Announcement
- Store page live with wishlist CTA
- 6 to 10 screenshots
- 30 to 60 second teaser trailer

### Vertical Slice window
- Weekly build updates
- One focused feature post per week (typing feedback, map, rewards, upgrades)

### Demo or Early Access
- Launch devlog
- Patch notes template
- Community FAQ refresh

## Content types
- Short clips (10 to 20 seconds) of typed interventions
- Screenshots of battle HUD and rewards screen
- One gif focused on accuracy or streak buffs

## Tracking fields
- date
- channel (Steam, itch, social)
- asset type (screenshot, clip, devlog)
- key message
- status
